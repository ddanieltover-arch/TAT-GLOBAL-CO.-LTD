## 5. Glutinous Rice (Sticky Rice)

**Slug:** `glutinous-rice`  
**Badge:** 🍚 Thai Specialty  
**Status:** In Stock  

**Short Description:**  
Traditional Thai sticky rice (khao niao) — opaque, short-grain, and intensely sticky when cooked. A staple of Lao, Thai, and Cambodian cuisines and specialty food markets worldwide.

**Full Description:**  
Thai Glutinous Rice (Khao Niao) is the traditional staple of Thailand's northeastern and northern regions. With near-zero amylose content (waxy starch), it cooks to a cohesive, sticky mass that is eaten by hand or used in sweets, desserts, and street food applications. TAT Global supplies white glutinous rice and black (purple) glutinous rice for specialty buyers.

## 6. Parboiled Rice

**Slug:** `parboiled-rice`  
**Badge:** 💪 Nutritional Choice  
**Status:** In Stock  

**Short Description:**  
Partially pre-cooked under steam pressure, then milled. Retains more nutrients than white rice, cooks firm and separate. Popular in South Asia, Middle East, and African markets.

**Full Description:**  
TAT Global's Parboiled Rice undergoes a steam-pressure process before milling, which drives nutrients from the bran into the grain and gelatinizes the starch. The result is a firmer, more nutritious product with longer shelf life, golden-amber color, and non-sticky cooking behavior. Ideal for markets that demand a separate, firm grain such as South Asian restaurants, Middle Eastern distributors, and African institutional buyers.

**Technical Specifications:**
| Attribute | Value |
|---|---|
| Process | Steam parboiled |
| Color | Light amber/golden |
| Broken Percentage | 5% / 25% |
| Moisture Content | ≤ 13.5% |
| Whiteness | N/A (amber grade) |
| Gelatinization | Fully gelatinized starch |
| Shelf Life | 18 months (vs 12 for white rice) |

**Markets:** India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Nigeria, Egypt, UAE, UK (South Asian diaspora)  
**Packaging Options:** 25kg, 50kg bags; custom  
**MOQ:** 20 MT
