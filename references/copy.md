## About Section

**Section Label:** About TAT Global  
**Headline:** A Trusted Name in Thai Rice Manufacturing Since Decades

**Body:**  
TAT GLOBAL CO., LTD is a Thailand-based bulk rice manufacturer and international distributor committed to delivering premium quality rice to buyers worldwide. Operating from the heart of Thailand's rice belt, we manage every step of the supply chain — from paddy sourcing and milling to export documentation and container loading.

With decades of experience in the Thai agricultural industry, we serve international importers, food distributors, supermarket chains, hospitality suppliers, and government procurement agencies across Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and the Americas.

Our facilities operate under internationally recognized food safety standards including GMP, HACCP, and ISO 22000. Every shipment is supported by full export documentation: Certificates of Analysis, Phytosanitary Certificates, Certificates of Origin, and Halal certification (where required).

## FAQ Page

**Q1:** What is the minimum order quantity?  
**A1:** Our standard MOQ is 20 MT (one 20ft container). For specialty varieties or private label, we can accommodate lower MOQs from 5–10 MT. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.

**Q2:** Do you offer private label packaging?  
**A2:** Yes. We offer a complete private label program including custom bag printing in any language, nutritional label formatting for your target market, and full compliance documentation. Lead time is approximately 45 days from artwork approval.

**Q3:** What certifications does your rice carry?  
**A3:** Our facilities are certified GMP, HACCP, and ISO 22000. Each shipment is supported by a Certificate of Analysis, Phytosanitary Certificate, and Certificate of Origin. Halal certification is available upon request.

**Q4:** What are your shipping terms?  
**A4:** We typically ship FOB from Laem Chabang or Bangkok Port. CIF terms are available upon request. We work with trusted freight forwarders globally.

**Q5:** How quickly can you respond to a quote request?  
**A5:** We aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent requirements, please indicate this in your message or contact us directly on WhatsApp.

**Q6:** Can you provide samples?  
**A6:** Yes, we can arrange product samples (typically 1–2 kg per variety) for qualified buyers. Sample shipping costs may apply depending on destination. Please contact our sales team.

**Q7:** What markets do you currently supply?  
**A7:** We currently supply buyers in 40+ countries across Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and the Americas. We are experienced with the import requirements and documentation of most major rice-importing countries.

**Q8:** How do I get started?  
**A8:** Simply click "Request a Quote" anywhere on this website, fill in your requirements, and our export team will be in touch within 24 hours. No commitment required.
